Building Better Healthcare

A healthcare technology solutions platform with a mission to address the inefficiencies of our healthcare industry and improve patient experience by leveraging AI and technology.

Founded in 2023 by two friends that witnessed the issues of a cumbersome and stagnant healthcare system first hand from a doctor and a patient’s perspective and saw the opportunities and potential that advancements in AI and technology could bring, they decided to match a problem to a solution.

Designed with the needs of healthcare providers and patients in mind, the platform features an AI medical assistant that combines advancements in natural language processing and machine learning with their in-house diagnostics algorithm, in addition to other solutions in pre & post consultation patient management, care and communications.

We aim to support healthcare professionals by

  • Providing pre-diagnostic analytics
  • Streamlining of workflows in:

Patient intake

Triaging

Diagnosis

Treatment

Post-consultation Care

At the same time, we improve patient experience and outcome in their otherwise painful healthcare journey

From finding relevant medical information,

matching healthcare providers

to dealing with administrative hurdles before and after their consultation.
